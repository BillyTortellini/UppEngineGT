Tactical Takeover
-----------------
First/3rd person island takeover simulator. 
Goal: Take over the whole island, island is made of multiple bases.
A limited amout of weapons, enemies and vehicles.
First you have to take over lower level bases, since these don't have
armored vehicles or stuff like that. Then you can take over big bases,
get better equipment...
